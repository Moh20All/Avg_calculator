About This Project

The Master Average Calculator was designed to help students at the University of Biskra easily calculate their semester grades. As a student myself, I experienced the challenge of manually calculating weighted averages across different courses, especially considering the varying weights and grading formulas used in different departments.

Features

  • Support for all Master's programs at the Department of Computer Science
  • Accurate calculations based on the official grading formulas
  • PDF export for easy sharing and printing of results
  • User-friendly interface with real-time feedback

Technologies Used

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Tailwind CSS jsPDF
